Want to know about Lab Rat Experiment?

Scientific Method Game!

Lab Rat Experiment is a mobile educational game designed to help Junior High School and Senior High School students master the scientific method through interactive gameplay. Set in a virtual laboratory, players explore six distinct levels that cover each step of the scientific method, from observation to experimentation and analysis. With a Bookworm-inspired narrative that drives discovery and problem-solving, students engage in hands-on virtual experiments such as chemical purification, microscope analysis, and pH testing. The game features interactive tutorials, gamified challenges, and feedback mechanisms‚Äîsuch as humorous lab rat responses‚Äîthat reinforce learning in an engaging and immersive way. Tailored for schools with limited access to physical labs, Lab Rat Experiment provides a dynamic, accessible learning experience that bridges theoretical knowledge with practical application, empowering students to develop both scientific skills and critical thinking in a fun and innovative way.

Mission

Our mission is to enhance the scientific literacy of Junior and Senior High School students by transforming complex scientific concepts into interactive learning experiences. Through Lab Rat Experiment, we aim to:

  • Empower learners to master the scientific method using gamified, discovery-based simulations.
  • Provide inclusive access to hands-on virtual experiments for schools with limited laboratory infrastructure.
  • Promote critical thinking, curiosity, and deeper conceptual understanding through a Bookworm-inspired educational framework.

Vision

To become a leading force in transforming science education by harnessing the power of gamification and technology, inspiring a new generation of learners to think critically, experiment fearlessly, and explore the world through the lens of scientific inquiry.
