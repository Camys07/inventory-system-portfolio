TUTORIAL

The tutorial introduces players to the virtual lab environment, guiding them through essential controls, equipment usage, and scientific procedures. It serves as a foundational walkthrough to help players confidently begin their experiments.

STARTING PAGE

The Starting Page provides access to the main game, a tutorial for new users, customizable options, and a logout feature for secure account management.

OPTION

The Options menu allows users to manage sound effects, adjust the volume, and view information about the project team.

SELECTION MODE

The Selection screen lets users choose between Singleplayer and Multiplayer game modes based on their preferred gameplay experience.

LEVEL STAGES

The Level Stages present a series of progressively challenging tasks that guide the player through different phases of the scientific method.

COTROLLER

The Controller section provides an overview of the game controls, mouse actions used to navigate, interact with tools, and perform lab tasks.

PROF. BLUE RAT

Prof. Blue Rat is the virtual mentor who assigns tasks, guides players through experiments, and validates their progress throughout the game.

ALMANAC STATION

The Almanac Station is an in-game reference hub where players can access information about lab tools, procedures, and scientific concepts to support their tasks.

CENTRIFUGE STATION

The Centrifuge Station is where players purify chemical as part of their laboratory experiments.

CHEMICAL MIX STATION

The Chemical Mix Station is a precision unit where purified chemicals are carefully transferred into a beaker for controlled experimentation or further processing.

TO-DO LIST

The to-do list is a simple, organized tool used to outline and prioritize tasks that need to be completed, helping manage time and productivity effectively.

INVENTORY

The inventory is a storage system where collected items are organized and kept for future use, tracking resources acquired during gameplay or activities.

BOT TOMSKY

Bot Tomsky is responsible for throwing items to players and assisting players in identifying the items they have collected throughout the game.

BUNSEN BURNER

The Bunsen burner is used to heat chemicals placed in a test tube during experiments.
